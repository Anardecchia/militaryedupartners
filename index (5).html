Disclosures & Compliance Controls

Present on the live site across the consumer path.

  • Top of every page: “A non-government, privately-sponsored website.”
  • Bottom of every page: “This is a private website that is not affiliated with the U.S. government, U.S. Armed Forces, or Department of Veterans Affairs.”
  • GI Bill® attribution: registered trademark of the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs, with link to benefits.va.gov/gibill.
  • DoD notice: “The appearance of U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) visual information does not imply or constitute DoD endorsement.”
  • Sponsored Results disclosure shown directly above the school list, with the “not all schools” disclaimer and a link to the VA comparison tool.
  • TCPA consent is an unchecked checkbox, captured at Step 4, and is not a condition of purchase.
  • Certification: Jornaya LeadiD and TrustedForm tokens captured on every submission.
  • Policies: Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions linked in the site footer.
