WHERE GRADUATES WORK

CAREERS ACROSS THE HEALTHCARE ECOSYSTEM

Healthcare is projected to add more jobs than any other sector through 2032 — and South University graduates are positioned to fill them.

REGISTERED NURSING

Hospital bedside, ICU, ER, OR, and specialty practice. RN-BSN and BSN pathways open leadership roles.

ADVANCED PRACTICE NURSING

Family Nurse Practitioner, Nurse Anesthetist, Nurse Educator — roles with autonomy and strong earnings.

ALLIED HEALTH

Medical assistants, OTAs, PTAs, and other direct-care roles that get you working quickly.

HEALTHCARE ADMINISTRATION

Hospital management, clinic operations, practice management, and health-system leadership.

PUBLIC HEALTH

Community health programs, epidemiology, health education, and policy work with government and nonprofits.

SPECIALIZED CLINICAL

Physician Assistant, Anesthesiologist Assistant, and pharmacy roles requiring advanced credentials.
