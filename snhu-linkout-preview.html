How the SNHU Linkout Works

  • The SNHU card appears at the bottom of the results on the homepage form only — not on any landing page forms (Business, Healthcare, IT, Trades)
  • It shows every time Step 5 (results) loads, regardless of how many LeadHoop school matches come back above it
  • The "Learn More" button is a LeadHoop redirect link that pre-fills the lead's first name, last name, phone, email, city, state, zip, and all UTM parameters automatically
  • Opens in a new tab so the user doesn't lose their matched school results
  • Respects test mode — passes test=true when the site's LeadHoop test mode toggle is on
  • The card above (Post University) is just an example of what a real LeadHoop match looks like — the SNHU card will always sit below whatever matches come back
