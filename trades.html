Why Trades?

Why Skilled Trades Fit Military Backgrounds

Your service gave you exactly what the trades industry values most — discipline, technical skill, and the ability to get things done.

Your hands-on training transfers directly

Military MOS experience in mechanics, engineering, and logistics maps closely to skilled trade certifications. Many programs offer credit for prior service training.

High demand, short programs

Most trade certifications take 6–18 months. The skilled trades face a massive labor shortage — job placement rates exceed 90% in many fields.

GI Bill covers it

VA-approved trade schools accept GI Bill and TA benefits. Many veterans complete programs with zero out-of-pocket cost.

Earn while you learn

Apprenticeship programs let you earn a paycheck while training. Average apprentice wages start at $15–$20/hr and increase with experience.
